# Future of Computer Engineering: The AI Era Analysis

This repository hosts a comprehensive research study on the transformation of **Computer Engineering** roles as AI continues to reshape the industry. By analyzing 100 distinct career paths, this project aims to provide a data-driven roadmap for engineers to navigate the next decade of technological evolution.

---

## 🚀 Overview

The "AI Era" is moving beyond simple software automation into hardware-level integration and autonomous systems. This research evaluates how traditional Computer Engineering roles (from Embedded Systems to VLSI) are merging with Artificial Intelligence.

### Project Highlights:
*   **100 Career Paths analyzed:** A deep dive into traditional, emerging, and hybrid roles.
*   **Scoring System:** Each path is scored based on growth potential, salary trends, and "AI-resistance."
*   **Research-Backed:** Data compiled using advanced research tools and industry market reports.

---

## 📊 The Scoring Methodology

To ensure objective results, I used a multi-layered research approach to score each career path (0-100):

1.  **AI Synergy:** How much the role benefits from AI integration.
2.  **Market Longevity:** Projected demand through 2035.
3.  **Skill Complexity:** The barrier to entry and specialized knowledge required.
4.  **Automation Risk:** The likelihood of the core tasks being fully automated.
